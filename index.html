Be Your Best Self 3D Project

Project

Be Your Best Self

Type

3D Cartoon

Date

1 January 2022

For

Arena Multimedia

A Boy, who work from home. He is busy with dozens of jobs. He looked tiredly at the pet dog lying in a corner. He picked up his phone to play and looked at the messy room with boredom at that time. And then he thought of a mirror. You clone a person just to clean the room, you work. He felt everything was fine, next he thought about cloning another person to walk the pet. And then add another person who does his job so that he can sit

and play on the phone. He felt so happy that one day he came home and all versions of him were playing on the phone just like him, sitting on his sofa, lying on his bed and the room was still as messy as ever. He was tired and got out of that thought, back to the reality of being alone with his pet dog. He happily continued his work.

TCB Fifa World Cup 2022

Type

Date

Client

The 2022 FIFA World Cup was an international football tournament contested by the men's national teams of FIFA's member associations and 22nd edition of the FIFA World Cup. It took place in Qatar from 20 November to 18 December 2022, making it the first World Cup held in the Arab world and Muslim world, and the second held entirely in Asia after the 2002 tournament in South Korea and Japan.

This tournament was the last with 32 participating teams, with the number of teams being increased to 48 for the 2026 edition. To avoid the extremes of Qatar's hot climate, the event was held during November and December. It was held over a reduced time frame of 29 days with 64 matches played in eight venues across five cities. Note: Key visual belongs to Dentsu Redder.
